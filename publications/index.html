2024

  1. Cartography
    Accessibility Mapping Methodologies Using OpenStreetMap: A Systematic Approach for Inclusive Urban Planning
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  Maria Silva ,  and  João Santos
    International Journal of Cartography, 2024
    Abs Bib

    This paper presents a comprehensive methodology for creating detailed accessibility maps using OpenStreetMap data and crowdsourced mapping techniques. We demonstrate how systematic data collection protocols can support inclusive urban planning initiatives and improve pedestrian navigation for people with mobility impairments.

  2. IEEE
    Machine Learning Approaches for Automated Sidewalk Detection in Urban Mobile Mapping Data
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  Jennifer Thompson ,  and  Li Wang
    IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, 2024
    Abs Bib

    We propose novel machine learning techniques for automated detection and classification of sidewalk infrastructure in mobile mapping point clouds. Our approach achieves high accuracy in identifying pedestrian pathways and accessibility features, supporting scalable urban accessibility mapping.

2023

  1. FOSS4G
    OSM SidewalKreator: A QGIS Plugin for Efficient Sidewalk Network Creation in OpenStreetMap
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  Ana Paula Lima ,  and  Roberto Costa
    In Proceedings of the Free and Open Source Software for Geospatial Conference , 2023
    Abs Bib PDF Code

    We present OSM SidewalKreator, an open-source QGIS plugin designed to streamline the creation of detailed sidewalk networks in OpenStreetMap. The tool automates geometry generation while ensuring compliance with OSM tagging standards for accessibility features.

  2. IJGIS
    Quality Assessment of Crowdsourced Accessibility Data in OpenStreetMap: A Case Study of Brazilian Cities
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  Carlos Oliveira ,  and  Patricia Mendes
    International Journal of Geographical Information Science, 2023
    Abs Bib

    This study evaluates the quality and completeness of accessibility-related data in OpenStreetMap across major Brazilian cities. We propose quality metrics and validation protocols for crowdsourced accessibility information, contributing to improved data reliability for navigation applications.

  3. Workshop
    Hands-on Workshop: Creating Accessible Maps with Open Source Tools
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena
    2023
    Abs

    This workshop introduces participants to open-source tools and methodologies for creating accessibility-focused maps. Participants learn to use QGIS, OpenStreetMap, and specialized plugins for comprehensive accessibility mapping workflows.

2022

  1. SBSR
    OpenSidewalkMap: Collaborative Platform for Comprehensive Pedestrian Network Mapping
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  Lucas Ferreira ,  and  Amanda Rodrigues
    In Simpósio Brasileiro de Sensoriamento Remoto , 2022
    Abs Bib PDF Website

    We present OpenSidewalkMap, a collaborative mapping initiative focused on creating detailed pedestrian network inventories. The platform combines remote sensing analysis, field surveys, and community mapping to support inclusive urban mobility planning.

2021

  1. RBCG
    Geodetic Reference Systems for Urban Accessibility Mapping: Precision Requirements and Best Practices
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena ,  and  Eduardo Silva
    Revista Brasileira de Cartografia, 2021
    Abs Bib

    This paper examines precision requirements for geodetic reference systems in urban accessibility mapping applications. We establish best practices for coordinate system selection and transformation procedures to ensure consistent and accurate accessibility datasets.

  1. Terrestrial Mobile Mapping for Urban Infrastructure Documentation: Applications in Accessibility Assessment
    Kauê de Moraes Vestena
    2020
    Abs Bib PDF

The research demonstrates how high-resolution geospatial data can support detailed accessibility assessments and inclusive urban planning initiatives.
